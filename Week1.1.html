Lapser project
Not just another rotating head

Before I start, I have to mention that pro-level stuff for making photo and video is far too expeisive for a student or just an amateur to buy and rotating heads (if they are better than a modificated egg-timer) are qualified as pro-level equipment. So, the only way is to produce one's own equipment.
So, what does a good rotating time lapse head require? (IMHO, of course) Fitst of all, all parameters as quantity of frames per minute, angle and totla time of shooting should be adjustable. Then, to take shots there has to be a wire sending a signal to shutter. And last but not least - it has to work on a single charge about 8 hrs.

To make it real I have to:

• Make a controller with step motor driver

• Make a gear to increase motor's torque

• Make a control panel to adjust shooting parametrs

• Make an adapter for camera and smartphone to mount it on rotating platform

• Make a housing to suit the whole thing
